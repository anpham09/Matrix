## About The Project

GenMatrix is a **2D educational platformer game** designed to teach students about:

- Cybersecurity and online safety
- Ethical applications of artificial intelligence
- Digital literacy in school, social media, and creative environments

The story follows a girl who becomes trapped inside a **generative AI matrix**, where players must navigate levels and answer questions to progress.

### Why GenMatrix?

As AI and digital tools become increasingly accessible to younger audiences, many students lack proper education on:

- Online privacy and safety
- How AI systems work
- Ethical technology usage

GenMatrix aims to bridge this gap through **interactive, game-based learning**, aligning with:

- **UN SDG 4:** Quality Education
- **UN SDG 10:** Reduced Inequalities

## Built Using

This project was developed using:

- **Godot Engine** — game development and programming
- **Kleki** — basic art and sprite creation

## Usage

GenMatrix is designed to be intuitive and accessible.

### Getting Started

- Launch the game  
- Accept the Privacy Policy and Terms of Use  
- Navigate to the home screen  
- Click **Start Game**  

### Gameplay Mechanics

- Explore levels in a 2D platformer environment  
- Encounter enemies that challenge you with questions  
- Answer questions related to:
  - AI concepts  
  - Cybersecurity  
  - Online safety  

### Rules

- Correct answer → continue  
- Incorrect answer → restart the level  
- End of level → complete a final quiz  

### Level Progression

- **Level 1:** Into the Matrix  
- **Level 2:** Square Roots  
- **Level 3:** Vivaldi  

Each level increases in difficulty and introduces more advanced concepts.

### Educational Goals

Players will learn:

- How to stay safe online  
- A basic understanding of AI  
- Responsible and ethical technology use
